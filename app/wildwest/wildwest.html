Wild West Shootout Game

Back in Spring of 2015, a group of 2 colleagues and I were tasked to make a project out of a PIC microcontroller. At the time, addressable LEDs where relatively new along with the ESP8266 WiFi-enabled microcontroller. We thought amongst ourselves and decided, why not make a game out of this? It took a while to do all of this and I'm more than glad that I paired up with Adam and Nick - we all carried the same passion for this project.

After a some hundreds of lines of code in assembly (our 56 page report was roughly 44 pages of code), we successfully demonstrated a fun game in which two players would "face off" and whoever "shot" first would win.
